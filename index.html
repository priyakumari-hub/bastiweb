About Us

At Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation, we are more than just an organization — we are a movement driven by the belief that education is the cornerstone of empowerment. Established under the Indian Societies Act of 1860, we stand as a beacon of hope in underserved communities.

Why Education Is Important

  • Breaking the Cycle of Poverty: Education offers slum children a pathway out of poverty with knowledge and skills.
  • Empowerment and Self-Improvement: It gives them confidence to dream and advocate for themselves.
  • Building Resilience and Hope: Education equips children with skills to overcome adversity.

We’re assembling a force for change. Join us — whether you're a teacher, organizer, or volunteer, there's a place for you. Join Us and be the change.
